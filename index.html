Our Love Story

The Story Begins

Dec 4, 2009

Anthony and Victoria grew up in the same boring small town of Hawthorne, NY. Unless beauty salons, pizzerias, and banks are your jam, not much really goes down in H-Town. So, a small boring town filled with marinara sauce and too much hairspray - not really the perfect starting off point for a love story (unless you consider The Nanny the love story of the century). So then, what happened? How did two awkward kids who went to school together since the first grade wade through all of the gabagool and fake nails to find each other? Well the answer is simple: I don't know. But, it happened.

It wasn't until junior year of high school where these two love birds met through mutual friends and finally mustered up the courage to talk to each other. So what does a group of 16 - 17 year olds do in such a vibrant and lively town such as Hawthorne on a Saturday night? They go to the local shopping center of course. Ah yes, Rose Hill: the epicenter of romance for any high school kid at Westlake. They even have a pizzeria, a beauty salon, and a bank all in the same shopping center so you don't have to drive 30 seconds to the other nearest one.

So after a few hours of being together with mutual friends, Victoria finally looked up from her phone - it actually might have been the first time Anthony saw her face. My god, was she nervous and awkward, but Anthony also found it endearing and adorable. So what does an equally awkward and nervous boy do to break the ice? A mock strip show on a street sign poll. How could she resist such a display, right? Well, I guess it ended up working for god knows what reason, because they started talking more and more. So much so, that Victoria barely looked at her phone when hanging out.

This went on a few more months until Anthony decided that he needed to make this girl his. So on December 4th, 2009 - a day after Victoria'said birthday. Anthony (finally) asked out the girl he knew he was going to marry one day.
